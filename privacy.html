Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

We keep this simple: we collect only what we need to run the service, we never sell your data, and you can contact us anytime to delete your information.

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Product Description Writer ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service at product-description-writer.vercel.app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Product information you enter — product names, features, and descriptions you type into our tool. This is used only to generate your listing.
  • Usage data — how many generations you have used, stored locally in your browser via localStorage. We do not store this on our servers.
  • Payment information — if you upgrade to Pro, your payment is processed by our payment provider. We do not store your card details directly.
  • Email address — if you contact us or sign up for Pro, we collect your email to manage your account and communicate with you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate AI-powered product descriptions using your inputs
  • Process your subscription payment and manage your Pro account
  • Respond to your support requests and questions
  • Improve and maintain our service

3. How We Process Your Data with AI

The product information you enter (product name, features, target customer) is sent to OpenAI's API to generate your product description. This means your input data is processed by OpenAI in accordance with their privacy policy. We do not store your inputs on our servers after the description is generated.

We encourage you not to enter personally identifiable information in the product fields.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only with:

  • OpenAI — to process your product description requests
  • Payment processors — to handle subscription payments securely
  • Legal authorities — if required by law

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to track your free generation count. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Our service is hosted on Vercel which provides secure HTTPS connections. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of any communications from us
  • Cancel your subscription at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at aiproductwritter@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at abhishek@gmail.com.
